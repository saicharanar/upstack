GSAP (the GreenSock Animation Platform) is the go-to for **precise, sequenced, imperative**
animation — timelines, eases, and scroll effects that would be awkward declaratively. It's not
React-specific, so you drive it through refs, wrapped safely in the `useGSAP` hook for automatic
cleanup.

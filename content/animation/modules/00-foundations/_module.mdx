Libraries like Motion and GSAP are powerful, but they sit on top of the browser's own animation
model — and the *principles* of good motion are the same whichever tool you use. This module
covers the foundation: how CSS animates, what makes motion feel good and run at 60fps, and how to
respect users who prefer less motion.

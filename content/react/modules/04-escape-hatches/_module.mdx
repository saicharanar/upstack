Most of the time you stay inside React's declarative model. But sometimes you need to hold a
value without re-rendering, touch the DOM directly, or synchronize with an external system.
These are the escape hatches — refs, Effects, and custom Hooks — plus the crucial skill of
knowing when you *don't* need them.
